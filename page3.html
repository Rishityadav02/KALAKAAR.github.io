Vignesh Moorthi

Vighnesh Moorthi | 23rd april, 2022

The homemade jewellery Artist

Vighnesh Moorthi is a visual artist and contemporary jewellery designer based in Manchester. Working predominantly with ceramics, she adapts and uses techniques and processes typically found in traditional ceramics to make abstract, playful, sculptural work for the body with a focus on line, form and colour. Informed by her visual arts practice, her jewellery is viewed as an expansion of her drawing, as all pieces are made using coils as a starting point - giving flow and movement to her pieces.
